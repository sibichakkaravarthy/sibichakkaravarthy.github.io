About Me

Sibi Chakkaravarthy Sethuraman obtained his Doctorate of Philosophy degree from Anna University in 2018. Presently, he serves as a Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Vellore Institute of Technology – Andhra Pradesh (VIT-AP) University. He is one of the youngest professors in VIT. During the period of 2019–2020, he held the position of a visiting professor and was affiliated with the Artificial Intelligence Lab at University Systems of New Hampshire (KSC campus), USA. Moreover, Dr. Sibi is the Coordinator of the Centre of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (AIR) at VIT-AP. He played a leading role as the primary engineer for the project VISU, an advanced 3D-printed humanoid robot developed by VIT-AP. Dr. Sibi actively contributes to the open-source community and holds the position of lead writer in renowned security magazines such as Pentestmag and eForensics. He also serves in key editorial roles, including Section Editor for Network Security, Edge & Cloud Computing in Telecommunication Systems journal (Springer, I.F – 2.3) and Associate Editor for SN Computer Science (Springer). Furthermore, his recent lecture series was featured and published in NPTEL. Dr. Sibi has also been honoured with the DST fellowship
